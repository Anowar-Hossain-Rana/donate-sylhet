Legacy Giving

Legacy gifts are a powerful way to provide for the future of Le Korsa
and its beneficiaries and to ensure that your values live on. A
legacy gift is a planned future donation that comes from your
estate, or a donor you represent. This can include bequests,
endowment gifts, stock transfers, charitable gift annuities,
property,charitable remainder trusts, life insurance policies,
or anything that you feel would help further our mission.
